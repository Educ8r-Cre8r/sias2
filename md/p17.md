\# üìö Core Concepts

1\. \*\*Light Reflection\*\*: When light bounces off a surface, such as glass or a mirror.  
2\. \*\*Refraction\*\*: The bending of light as it passes through different materials, like glass.  
3\. \*\*Transparency and Opacity\*\*: How materials allow light to pass through (transparent) or block it (opaque).

\# üìñ Vocabulary

\- \*\*Reflection\*\*: The bouncing of light off a surface.  
\- \*\*Refraction\*\*: The bending of light as it passes through different materials.  
\- \*\*Transparent\*\*: Allowing light to pass through easily, like glass or water.  
\- \*\*Opaque\*\*: Blocking light from passing through, like wood or metal.
